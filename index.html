Technical Report

Designed and Developed an extension to Fastron, a kernel-perceptron based proxy collision checking algorithm. The previous method randomly sampled around support vectors to predict their new locations. We developed an equation to relate obstacle motion to support vector translation using inverse kinematics of a subsection of the robot arm. With the new heuristic, collision accuracy increased by 20%. Developed an Unscented Kalman Filter based tracking algorithm to track obstacles. To accommodate for the noise in observation and tracking, I modified the original Fastron training algorithm by formulating support vectors as random variables.
